THE SECRET OF A REALLY
GOOD FIRST DATE

SCAN #3


YOU CAN MASTER FIRST DATES ALL YOU LIKE
BUT YOU SHOULD KNOW THIS

There is no such thing as truly and formidably mastering a first date, but you can certainly do better than usual. You can make all the eye contact you want, listen and share, not talk too much but not too little, read the map and endevour for some warmth. However, at the end it may be a feeling you have within you and them, something inexplicable, magical and just so clear. You would simply know. You would know if they are interested in you too, you would know there shall be a second time, you would feel a connection. You would even know this if you aren't a master of first dates, more so terrible at it.
