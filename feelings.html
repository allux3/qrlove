SCAN #4


HERE'S HOW I GOT TO THE NOTION
OF DISASSMBLYING FEELINGS FROM
SOMEONE INSTRUCTION NOTEBOOK

I had feelings for someone obviously, except they weren't too much to do anything with. I mean, nothing is ever black and white or 0% and 100%, we live in a bundle of colorful grey areas. So I decided I shall put my feelings on psychological steroids and make them even more than what they were. Intentionally for creating this furniture inspired instruction book. How can I use a platform that shows you very clearly with no error how to assemble a closet or a coffee table in order to do the opposite.
To disassemble. But something so vague and unmeasurable? Obviously that is not the greatest way to get over someone, these things are natural and there is no specimen. So I wanted to create a formula given the notion there might not be one. That is what I came up with.
